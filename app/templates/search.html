About the filter limitations
  • Remote: Adzuna has no native remote flag. We append "remote" to your keywords and then post-filter results whose title/description/location mentions remote / WFH / telecommute / distributed. Some hybrid roles will slip through.
  • H1B-friendly: No public API tells us who sponsors. We match against a curated list of ~200 top historical LCA filers (FY23/24 DOL data). Many startups and mid-size firms sponsor without being on the list. The "↗ visa history" link next to every employer takes you to myvisajobs.com for that exact company.
  • Employer size: Not available from Adzuna. Each result has a "↗ LinkedIn" link so you can check headcount yourself.
  • Salary: Adzuna shows ranges where employers publish them; "(est)" means Adzuna's model predicted the band from comparable roles.
